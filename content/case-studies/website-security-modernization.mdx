## The Challenge

A logistics and storage company's WordPress site was compromised. Hackers had injected a betting platform into the database, turning the client's legitimate business website into a front for illicit content. The client needed urgent help — their business operations and reputation were at risk.

## The Approach

Before making any changes, I accessed the wp-admin panel to fully understand the existing site and what the client wanted to preserve. During this audit I discovered the breach was deeper than expected — multiple backdoors, compromised plugins, and injected database entries.

## The Solution

- Identified all backdoors and entry points used by the attacker
- Cleaned every compromised file and database record
- Rebuilt the entire site as a headless WordPress + Next.js application
- Developed a custom security plugin to replace the vulnerable one
- Ran a comprehensive security audit on the completed work
- Added PWA support with push notifications so the client's customers could get a mobile app experience for quick updates

## Results

- **Client reclaimed their business** — able to run operations peacefully
- **Zero security incidents** since the rebuild
- Customers gained a PWA mobile experience with real-time notifications
- The client can now focus on their shipment tracking and storage vault business with confidence
